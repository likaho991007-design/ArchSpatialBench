ArchSpatialBench

A multimodal benchmark under development for spatial reasoning on architectural drawings, including floor plans, sections, and elevations.

Research Question

Can multimodal AI models coordinate architectural drawings across plan, section, and elevation, or do they rely on language priors and visual templates?

Benchmark Structure

LevelFocusRole
L1Basic drawing semanticsPerception sanity check
L2Single-drawing spatial relationsSpatial reasoning baseline
L3Cross-drawing integrationMain capability target
L4Geometric precisionPrecision ceiling probe

Diagnostic Conditions

ConditionPurpose
VStandard visual input with drawing and question
TText-only blind baseline for language-prior shortcuts
T+DStructured textual description versus visual interpretation
V+WWrong-image resistance
V+RRotation consistency

Data Strategy

Core Data is the redistributable benchmark split, planned around license-compatible plan / section / elevation matched sets.

Wild Data is the URL-only in-the-wild evaluation split. ArchDaily-hosted source images are not redistributed.

We will not scrape, bulk-download, or redistribute ArchDaily-hosted content without written permission.
